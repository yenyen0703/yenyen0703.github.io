Donutbit FAQ

What is Donutbit?

Donutbit is a minesweeper like game powered by Moneypot. To win the game, players must select Sweet Donuts instead of the Toasted ones from the boxes provided. A player can get a reward (bit) for every Sweet Donut selected depending on the bet and on the number of Toasted traps that s(he) have chosen. The player can select as many boxes as (s)he like consecutively as long as (s)he is hitting Sweet Donuts, but the game will immediately end when the player hit even a single Toasted Donut.

What is the House Edge?

The house edge when betting on Donutbit is only 1%.

How can I be sure that bets are fair?

Donutbit has a probably fair system. Players can verify every single bet by simply clicking the Bet ID to prove that it’s not manipulated by anyone.

How do Legendary Donuts work?

Players must wager 500 bits or more at once to be listed automatically in the Legendary Donuts List. There is a maximum of 6 players to be listed, please note that if it exceeded, recent wagers will always replace the oldest spot.

Is the site mobile friendly?

Yes, Donutbit is playable on mobile devices (phone or tablet). We make sure that players will enjoy playing even on the go.

What is the max payout?

There is no maximum payout in the site. You can withdraw any amount you desired and it will go smoothly in your Moneypot Account.

What is moneypot?

MoneyPot is a bitcoin web wallet that lets you manage your funds across many MoneyPot casinos. Or maybe you just want to use MoneyPot as nothing more than a web wallet. That's cool, too. To know how moneypot works, read more here: https://www.moneypot.com/faq
